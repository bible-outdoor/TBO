About The Bible Outdoor (TBO)

The Bible Outdoor (TBO) is a spiritual movement to bring people closer to the Word of God in open environments. Our mission is to create a vibrant, welcoming community that worships God in Spirit and in Truth, studies the Bible together, and supports one another in faith and life.
